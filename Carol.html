Bodywork With

Carol Syvret Austin

Bodywork isn’t a type of massage. Bodywork is an alternative to massage. Our Intention is to restore mobility to your joints and muscles. We do this by breaking up scar tissue and rehydrating the connective tissues. Connective tissue is the binding and wrappings of all the muscles and the support structure for the organs and joints. Connective tissue is a liquid matrix. Repetitive strain, trauma, injury or surgery cause dehydration, micro tears, scars or rotational patterns in our connective tissue. All of these cause dysfunction. Dysfunction looks like tight muscles, pain, stiff achy joints, inability to walk barefoot, can’t bend down to lift my grandkids, can’t kneel on the floor with my kids because my knees hurt, can’t carry my golf clubs because my feet hurt. More recently I’ve been increasing mobility and range of motion in shoulders.
